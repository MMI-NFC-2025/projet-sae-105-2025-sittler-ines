Timeline

Discover other exceptional women scientists whose contributions have been minimized or attributed to others.

Marie Meurdrac

~1610 – ~1680

Chemistry

First woman to publish a chemistry treatise in France

Émilie du Châtelet

1706 – 1749

Physics and Mathematics

Translation of Newton's Principia, work on energy

Lise Meitner

1878 – 1968

Nuclear Physics

Co-discovery of nuclear fission (Nobel awarded to her colleague)

Rosalind Franklin

1920 – 1958

Molecular Biology

Discovery of DNA structure (credit given to Watson and Crick)

Katherine Johnson

1918 – 2020

Mathematics

Essential calculations for NASA's space missions

Jocelyn Bell Burnell

1943 –

Astrophysics

Discovery of pulsars (Nobel awarded to her thesis supervisor)
